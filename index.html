PAG-IBING NAGA ALIMBUKAD — Receipt Generator

This is the launcher page for the receipt generator. You can open the generator (the HTML file) or visit the linked Google Sheet that stores submissions and contains the Google Apps Script.

Open Receipt Generator Open Google Sheet

How to use

  1. Open Pag-Ibing Naga-Alimbukad.html by clicking Open Receipt Generator.
  2. In the app, paste your Google Apps Script Web App URL into the Google Apps Script URL field (top of the configuration section).
  3. Fill receipt details, then press Done (Fill & Send to Sheets). Data will be posted to the Google Sheet linked above.
